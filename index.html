Sushi

It is a sushi in the shape of a roll. The rice is put on the seaweed sheet (nori) and rolled. Inside there are ingredients like kampyo (a kind of dried vegetable), cucumber etc. To have the shape of a roll, there is a bamboo "makisu" tool.
